* Crypto Chatbot
* Portfolio Tracker

* Autonomous Market Research Agent
* Market Intelligence Platform
* Narrative Discovery Engine
* Alpha Discovery System

# NEW MODULE

## Alpha Score™

Every hypothesis must receive an Alpha Score.

Purpose:

Create a proprietary intelligence metric.

Formula inputs:

* Narrative Strength
* Historical Similarity
* Sentiment Alignment
* Technical Confirmation
* Volume Confirmation

Output:

0 - 100

Example:

Hypothesis Confidence: 91%

Alpha Score: 87

---

# NEW REQUIRED FIELD

## Why Now?

Every generated hypothesis must contain:

Why Now?

This section explains why the opportunity exists at this moment.

Example:

"AI Infrastructure narratives are accelerating while capital rotates away from speculative meme assets and into utility-focused ecosystems."

Purpose:

Increase explainability.

Increase investor appeal.

Improve demo quality.

---

# NEW REQUIRED FIELD

## Invalidating Conditions

Every hypothesis must contain:

What would make this thesis wrong?

Example:

* Narrative momentum drops
* BTC dominance exceeds 65%
* Volume contracts significantly
* Sentiment deteriorates

Purpose:

Show intellectual honesty.

Reduce AI hype.

Increase credibility.
